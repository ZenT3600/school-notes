## Formule

$$ V = I \times R $$

$$ P = I^2 \times R $$

$$ R_{EQ_{serie}} = \sum{R_i} $$

$$ R_{EQ_{parallelo}} = \frac{1}{\sum{\frac{1}{R_i}}} $$

$$ C_{EQ_{serie}} = \frac{1}{\sum{\frac{1}{C}}} $$

$$ C_{EQ_{parallelo}} = \sum{C} $$

In un nodo:

$$ \sum{I} = 0 $$

In una maglia:

$$ \sum{V} = 0 $$
